### 1. Executive Summary: The "Growth vs. Cost" Paradox
- Performance: Total Sales reached $847K, with a healthy 3.3% MoM growth.

- Efficiency Gap: While revenue is up, Total Orders dropped by 5.7%. This indicates a shift toward higher-value transactions (increased AOV) but suggests a potential struggle with customer acquisition volume.

- The Global Shift: International sales now represent 45% of total revenue, a massive jump that is currently the primary engine of growth but also the primary driver of rising costs.
